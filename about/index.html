About the Club

Locally Owned. Rake-Free. Yours.

Deuces Wild Poker Club is a legal, private poker room in Huntsville, Texas, owned and run by Mike & Ike. Four tables, forty seats, professional dealers, and never a dollar of house rake.

Why We're Here

A real card room, run by real people

We built Deuces Wild because Huntsville and East Texas deserved a poker room that's legal, fair, and friendly — not a back-room game, not a 90-minute drive to Houston, and not a corporate casino floor where you're just a seat number. Mike & Ike are local, they're at the room most nights, and they run it the way they'd want to be treated as players: no rake, clear rules, and a square deal every hand.

The longer story: we're finishing the founders' write-up and photos. For now, the short version is the honest one — two local guys who love the game built the room they wished existed. Meet Mike & Ike →

The Community

The people make the place

Our regulars come from Huntsville, the Lake Livingston towns, and as far as the north-Houston suburbs. Some have played for decades; plenty walked in for the first time intimidated and stayed because nobody made them feel dumb. Tournament nights have a buzz; cash games have that easy back-and-forth of people who actually like each other. Whoever you are, you're welcome at the table.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the more common questions asked about our poker club.

Do y’all have tournaments or what?

Yes — Deuces Wild Poker Club in Huntsville, Texas runs live poker tournaments multiple nights a week featuring Texas Hold’em, Omaha, and Big O (Hi/Lo). Games vary between tournaments and cash games depending on the night.

For a full schedule of tournament follow us on Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/huntsvillepoker

Are there prizes?

Yes — players compete for real cash prizes that come from tournament buy-ins. The payout structure is clearly posted for each event before the game starts.

How do you become a member?

To become a member, simply come to the club with a valid ID and sign up. You’ll pay the membership fee, and then you can buy into any tournaments or games running that day.

Do y’all do weekly tournaments?

Yes — Deuces Wild hosts tournaments every week including Texas Hold’em and Omaha. Some nights are themed events like College Night or Pot Limit Omaha Wednesdays.

Visit https://www.facebook.com/huntsvillepoker for the full schedule. 

What is a freeze-out tournament?

A freeze-out means once you lose all your chips, you’re out of the tournament — no rebuys or add-ons.

What is a rebuy tournament?

In a rebuy tournament, players can buy more chips if they go broke (usually within a set time frame). This keeps the action going and builds a bigger prize pool.

What city is Deuces Wild Poker Club located in?

Deuces Wild Poker Club is located in Huntsville, Texas, just a few minutes from Sam Houston State University. We’re proud to be part of the East Texas poker community and welcome players from Huntsville, Conroe, College Station, and beyond.

What type of poker games are offered?

Deuces Wild Poker Club offers a mix of Texas Hold’em, Omaha, and Big O (Hi/Lo). We rotate formats throughout the week, including freeze-outs, rebuys, and bounty tournaments. The schedule changes weekly, so check our Facebook page for the latest events.

Does Deuces Wild allow beginners or first-timers?

Absolutely. Beginners are always welcome at Deuces Wild. Our dealers and regulars are friendly and happy to explain the rules or help you learn. Whether you’re new to poker or just new to the area, we’ll make sure you feel at home.

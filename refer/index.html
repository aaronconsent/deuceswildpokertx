How It Works

You Both Win at the Door

1. Tell us who

Drop your name and your friend's name and mobile number in the form. That's it.

2. They come play

Your friend walks in any night we're open — Wednesday through Saturday. No reservation, no membership to try it out.

3. You both get rewarded

When your friend plays their first session, you both get a reward at the door. Rewards change from time to time — ask us about the current bring-a-friend reward.

Rewards are set by the owners and can change — ask Mike or Ike about the current bring-a-friend reward when you arrive.

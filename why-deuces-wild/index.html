Inside The Room

What The Room Actually Feels Like

Four tables, forty seats. Professional dealers on every hand. Regulars who know your name by the second night, and owners — Mike & Ike — on-site most nights to greet you at the door and settle anything that comes up.

The tournament structure is posted plainly and runs the same way every time: $60 buy-in / 18,000 starting chips, unlimited $40 rebuys (18,000 each) until the break, a $40 add-on at the break for 20,000 more chips, an optional $20 Last Longer, and an optional $5 High Hand. Hold'em tournaments Wednesday at 7 PM and Saturday at 6 PM.

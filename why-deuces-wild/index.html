How much is rake really costing you?

A lot more than you think. The drip of a few dollars per hand adds up to hundreds in a single session — money that comes straight out of your bankroll whether you win or lose.

Run the numbers. A typical raked room takes around $7 per hand, and a healthy cash table sees roughly 30 hands per hour. That's about $210 an hour pulled off the table by the house. Sit for a four-hour session and you've watched over $800 disappear into the rake box — and the table has collectively paid even more across all the seats.

At Deuces Wild, every dollar of that stays in play. We're 100% rake-free. The house never takes a cut of any pot, on any game, ever. Here's why that's also the legal foundation of how we operate.

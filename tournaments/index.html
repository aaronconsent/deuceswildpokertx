What's the Tournament Structure?

$60 to enter for 18,000 chips, with rebuys and an add-on to keep you in the hunt. Everything's posted up front — here it is.

ItemCostChipsNotes
Buy-In$6018,000Your entry & starting stack
Rebuy$4018,000Unlimited until the break
Add-On$4020,000One-time, offered at the break
Last Longer$20—Optional side bet
High Hand$5—Optional bonus
How the rebuys work: bust before the break and you can rebuy for $40 to get another 18,000 chips — as many times as you need until the break is called. At the break, everyone can take the one-time $40 add-on for 20,000 more chips.
The optional extras: the $20 Last Longer is a side pot among players who opt in, paid to whoever survives longest. The $5 High Hand is a bonus for the highest qualifying hand of the night. Both are completely optional — skip them if you like.

Rake-free, always: the house never takes a cut of the prize pool. Optional fees fund their own pots. Here's why that matters →

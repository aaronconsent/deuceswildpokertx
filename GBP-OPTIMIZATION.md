# Google Business Profile (GBP) — Optimization Checklist

For most local businesses the **Google Business Profile is a bigger ranking
asset than the website**, especially for "poker near me" / "poker huntsville tx"
searches that happen on phones. Work this list top to bottom. Manage it at
**business.google.com** (the "Deuces Wild Poker Club" profile).

> Keep the business name, address, and phone (**NAP**) byte-for-byte identical
> to the website everywhere: **Deuces Wild Poker Club · 120 FM 2821 Rd E,
> Huntsville, TX 77320 · (936) 000-0000** *(swap in the real phone)*.

## 9. Reviews — the #1 local ranking + trust factor
**Goal: more recent reviews than any other poker room in the region.**
- [ ] Use the **review-request flow** built into the site: open **`/admin/review.html`** on your phone after a good night and one-tap a text to the player with your Google review link. (Set your review link there once — see below.)
- [ ] Print the **review QR** from that page and put it at the host stand / on table tents so players can review on the spot.
- [ ] Short public link to hand out / put on cards: **deuceswildpokertx.com/review** (redirects to your Google review form).
- [ ] **Reply to every review** — thank the good ones by name; respond calmly and factually to any negative one. Reply velocity is a ranking signal too.
- [ ] Never buy/incentivize reviews (against Google policy; risks removal).

### How to get your Google review link (do this once)
1. On the GBP dashboard, click **"Ask for reviews"** / **"Get more reviews"** — Google gives you a short link like `https://g.page/r/XXXXXXXX/review`.
2. (Or) find your **Place ID** at https://developers.google.com/maps/documentation/places/web-service/place-id and use `https://search.google.com/local/writereview?placeid=YOUR_PLACE_ID`.
3. Paste that link into **`/admin/review.html`** (the "Review link" field) — it saves it and powers the one-tap text, the QR, and the /review/ short link.

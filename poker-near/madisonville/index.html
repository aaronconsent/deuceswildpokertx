Why Players Drive In From Madisonville

A Real Card Room Up The Road

Madisonville sits up I-45 north of Huntsville, which means the trip to Deuces Wild is a simple half-hour run south on the interstate from downtown Madisonville. For a town this size, having a legal private poker club just thirty minutes down the road is a genuine convenience — Deuces Wild operates under Texas Penal Code §47.02, with four tables, forty seats, and professional dealers at every game.

The drive ends at a room set up for players. The club is locally owned by Mike and Ike, who are usually on-site most nights. Texas Hold'em and Omaha cash games run every night the doors are open, with Texas Hold'em tournaments Wednesday at 7 PM and Saturday at 6 PM. The crowd is friendly and the seating is easy. Walk in any night we're open — there's no reservation and no membership needed to come find out how it plays.
